Our History

Started in 2010, MusicRoom quickly established itself as a Music Store icon par excellence in Nepal. With its unique offering of world fusion Music, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Nepal.

The store traces its humble beginnings to Musical Notes Store, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Johnny Chang, which offered music of different genre to its customers.
